Servicing the Washington Metropolitan Area

Calvert Contracting LLC is a full service commercial and light residential contracting company. We have over 20 years of experience and offer professional landscaping, general construction, building maintenance, commercial maintenance, and other general contracting services throughout Maryland. We pride ourselves on the quality of our craft, and the effective and efficient manner in which all jobs are completed. Calvert Contracting is available for anything you need, providing quality service no matter the demands of our clients. Get in touch today to set an appointment or for a free estimate.
